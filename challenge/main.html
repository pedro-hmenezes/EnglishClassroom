Question 1: Grammar

Which sentence is grammatically correct?

Correct Answer: "They are playing soccer now."
Explanation: This sentence uses the Present Continuous tense correctly for an action happening now. The other options have subject-verb agreement errors.

Question 2: Vocabulary

What is the synonym of "happy"?

Correct Answer: "Joyful."
Explanation: "Joyful" means feeling, expressing, or causing great pleasure and happiness.

Question 3: Prepositions

My birthday is ___ October.

Correct Answer: "in."
Explanation: We use the preposition "in" for months, years, and seasons.
